Noodles

Description

This is a simple vegetarian noodle recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chinese noodles
  • bok choy
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • 1 egg
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper powder
  • salt
  • chili oil
  • olive oil
  • sesame seed oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the egg (either fry it or hardboil it).
  2. Put water in a medium pot and put it on the stove to boil.
  3. Wash and chop the bok choy.
  4. Saute the bok choy using the olive oil.
  5. When the water is boiling, put in the noodles for a 4-5 minutes or as the package directs.
  6. Remove the noodles and put in a bowl. Put all the seasonings in the bowl, eyeballing the quantities.
  7. Add the bok choy and egg.
