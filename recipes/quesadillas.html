Quesadillas

Description

This is a vegetarian quesadilla recipe that is pretty light on spice.

Ingredients

  • tortillas
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 large onion
  • shredded Mexican cheese
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the onion.
  2. Mix the onion with the corn and black beans in a big bowl.
  3. Add all the spices to the mixture, eyeball the quantities.
  4. Heat up a pan.
  5. Lay one tortilla in the pan. Sprinkle shredded cheese over half the tortilla.
  6. On the same half, spoon a thin layer of the onion mixture over the cheese.
  7. Add another layer of cheese on the same half.
  8. Fold the tortilla in half.
  9. Once the tortilla is structurally sound enough, flip the quesadilla onto its other side.
  10. When both sides are browned, remove the quesadilla.
  11. Repeat until you have enough quesadillas.
